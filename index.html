DIGITAL DIARY - A day in my life

Not everyday is extraordinary, yet every single one leaves with quiet lessons behind.

I started my day with the most beautiful sound of our muazin. It was one of those days when you feel that life is beautiful without any specific reason and understand that it won't last like this forever. It was another ordinary day; I prayed fajr and got ready for school.
On my way to school, I found everyone and evrything calmer-even the air-and I began to realise that we see the reflection of ourselves in every single thing around us.
After I got to class, everything started to flow as usual. I spent hours at my desk writing, working on my projects, reading and even playing with friends. Within just a few hours, it was already time to go home, so I left as all my classmates did.
After a lonely trip -which was another beautiful addition to my day- I was at home asking my sister for some water to drink.
The rest of the day went as follows: after a short break, I tried to get back to work and do my homework while watching a movie -this is my favorite part of the day. The last hours of the day were spent with my family.
Even though it was another typical, ordinary day, I felt its warmth deep inside.

Jan-25-2026
Addis Ababa
